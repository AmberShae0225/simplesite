A Few Words About Me

I am a marketing and management student at the University of Northern Colorado. I am also minoring in psychology and will be continuing my education through the pursuit of my MBA upon graduation. I currently work as an Assistant Store Manager for Follet Hogher Education at the UNC Bookstore. When I am not in class or working, I enjoy spending time with my children, hiking, yoga, going to movies, and playing video games.

Future Career Interests

My future goals include purusing a career in digital marketing and eventually marketing management. I hope to continue gaining management experience through my current employment with Follett Higher Education while pursuing my education. Then I hope to move into a digital marketing role either within Follett or in a larger corporation within the Colorado area.

My Story of Growth

Another goal that I have is to use my unique story to help others along their path to healing and growth. Before beginning my educational goal I spent nearly a decade of my life struggling with drug addiction. When I finally found my way to being clean, I was in my late 20s and pregnant with my second son. Since then I have gotten myself into college, maintained a 3.5 and above GPA, found rewarding employment, and done all of this while raising my younger son on my own. I hope to inspire others in recovery by showing them that with hard work and time it is possible to build a life that they can be proud of. Anything is possible when we believe in ourselves and are willing to work for it!

More About Me

I have two awesome children, Harley and Troy. I have recently found that I have a love for traveling, which I hope to be able to share with them. I enjoy listening to podcasts and am currently engaged in a Wardlings campaign with my oldest son and other members of our family!
